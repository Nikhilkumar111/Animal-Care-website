Compassion for strays Isn't Optional - It's Overdue.

Stray Dog and Cat Population

Approximately 62 million stray dogs and 9.1 million stray cats are found across India.

Social Public Visibility

77% of Indians encounter stray dogs weekly, while 68% see stray cats regularly.

Homelessness of Companion Animals

Around 85% of companion animals in India are homeless.

Zoonotic Disease Risk

Stray animals contribute to the spread of zoonotic diseases like rabies, posing public health threats.

Population Control

Methods like TNR and contraception are used but face infrastructure challenges.

Awareness and Access

Awareness about animal welfare is growing, but consistent veterinary care is limited.

Our Mission

We are committed to rescuing, rehabilitating, and protecting animals in distress, ensuring they receive the care they deserve. Our efforts include swift emergency rescues, life-saving medical treatment, and long-term rehabilitation to give animals a second chance at life. Beyond immediate aid, we focus on raising public awareness to foster a compassionate society where animals are free from abuse, neglect, and suffering.

Our Values

Compassion

We believe in showing empathy and care for every animal, recognizing that their lives are as valuable, meaningful, and precious as our own.

Community

We understand that lasting change comes from collective effort and work closely with communities to raise awareness and educate on animal rights.

Integrity

We uphold transparency and honesty in all our operations, ensuring that every donation and effort is put to its best use for the welfare of animals.

AnimalCare

Serving and protecting our stray companions with love and care.
