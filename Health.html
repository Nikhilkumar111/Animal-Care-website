Health Tips for Stray Animals

Taking care of stray animals' health is vital for their well-being and public safety. Here are some basic tips to follow:

  • 📌 Always provide clean drinking water.
  • 🩺 If injured or sick, contact a local animal rescue NGO or vet.
  • 💉 Vaccinate against rabies and other diseases.
  • 🍲 Feed them nutritious food like rice, milk, or dog food.
  • 🚫 Avoid giving sweets, spicy food, or chocolate.
  • 🔄 Encourage sterilization to control overpopulation humanely.

If you're unsure how to help, reach out to a local NGO. Even small efforts can make a big difference!
